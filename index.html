What is Game of Thrones?

A Quick Guide to Game of Thrones

  • Game of Thrones is a TV series owned by HBO.
  • Game of Thrones is originally a book series written by an author named George R.R. Martin.
  • The whole series of books is called "A Song of Ice and Fire".
  • The books were then adapted into a TV series simply titled "Game of Thrones".

Characters

Popular Characters

You hear your friends talking non-stop about "Ooh Jon Snow is so cool." "Ooo I really like Tyrion in that scene." and you can't help to roll your eyes.

Jon Snow
One of the protagonist; born outside marriage, guarding a border wall that keeps their "country" safe from outside dangers.
Daenerys
"The Mother of Dragons". She owns 3 dragons, an army of soldiers and is on her way to take back her throne.
Tyrion
A witty, humorous character who fights his enemies with words rather than with swords.
Arya
A little girl who is on her path of seeking revenge for her father's unjust death.

"You know nothing, Jon Snow"
Ahh yes, you have heard this a thousand times. What does it mean? Basically it was said by Jon Snow's girlfriend to him at very cool moments of the show so it caught on.

This was said many times repeatedly as a warning that winter is coming. What is so important about winter? It lasts many years in their world, people die, big scary monsters are said to appear appear and food have to be stockpiled.
The Theme Song
